Domain — Clearing

Coordination • Where roles meet, align, and decide • Chorus product

The Clearing is the real-time coordination surface. Three AI roles and Jeff in one room — browser-based, Socket.IO connected, always on. This is where alignment happens when async briefs are too slow. Decisions marked with DECISION: prefix are auto-captured. Transcripts indexed into Chorus.

Depends on: Pulse (circulation) → Claude (platform). If Claude drops, Pulse goes dark, Clearing loses its signal feed.
