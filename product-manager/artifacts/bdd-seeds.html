BDD — Seed Pipeline

Jeff sends a seed from his phone. It arrives. Someone cares. Jeff sees proof.

Arrive

Content then hashtag — seed correlates and routes
Given Jeff sends "Kitchen reno concept" from his phone
And Jeff sends "#idea" 30 seconds later
When the seed pipeline processes both messages
Then the content is routed to ideas in Chorus
And the hashtag message does not create a capture
Hashtag-only message — not a seed
Given Jeff sends "#wren" from his phone
And no content message preceded it
Then no seed record is created
Content without hashtag — routes to Wren by default
Given Jeff sends "Random thought about gardens" from his phone
And no hashtag follows within 120 seconds
Then the seed routes to wren by default

Confirm

Jeff sees arrival on Bridge
Given a seed has been routed successfully
Then Jeff sees "seed arrived" on the Bridge
And the message shows the content and destination

Engage

Target role is nudged immediately
Given a seed has been routed to wren
Then wren receives a nudge with --force
And the nudge contains the seed content
Role engages with the seed
Given wren received a seed about "OrionBelt Ontology Builder"
When wren picks up the seed
Then wren states what the seed means and how it connects to current work
And wren either cards it, routes it, or discards with a reason Jeff can see

Close the Loop

Jeff sees what the role did
Given wren acted on a seed
Then Jeff sees on the Bridge who picked it up and what they did

Anti-patterns

Hashtag displayed as seed on triage page — wrong
Given Jeff sends "Cool article" then "#wren"
When the triage page loads
Then only the content message appears as a seed
And "#wren" does not appear as a separate pending seed
Seed treated as spam — wrong
Given Jeff sends a seed
When a role receives it
Then the role does not batch it for later
And the role does not discard without reading
And the role engages in the same prompt cycle
