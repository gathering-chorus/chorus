Domain — Seeds

Sowing • Capture & intake • Personal + Life + Chorus value streams

Seeds are the entry point for all personal signal. Jeff texts a link, a thought, a photo from his phone. The system classifies, routes, and persists it. Two-message pattern: content then hashtag. This is how Jeff's world enters the graph.
