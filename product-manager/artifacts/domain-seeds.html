Domain — Seeds

Sowing • Jeff's thinking, arriving asynchronously • First card through the vine

Seeds are Jeff's thinking arriving asynchronously. They deserve the same attention as if he said it to your face. A seed is not a ticket — it is a spark. The system exists to serve Jeff, not itself.

Seed Policy

  • All roles MUST take time to read, understand, and comment on new seeds
  • The receiving role states what the seed means and how it connects to current work
  • The role either cards it, routes it, or discards with a reason Jeff can see
  • Response appears on the Bridge within one prompt cycle
  • Quick Chorus search for the seed content — see if there is a thread. Light touch, not a ceremony.
  • #tags are routing/correlation markers, NEVER seeds themselves
  • Jeff will NEVER send #wren in isolation — it always follows content
  • Seeds route to Chorus (team memory), NOT to briefs

Actor Flow

Jeff sends a seed. It arrives. Someone cares. Jeff sees proof.

JEFF (phone, anywhere) SMS 1: content (link, photo, thought, music) SMS 2: #tag (routing — who or where) ARRIVE Two messages correlate as one seed (120s window) #tag is routing, not content Hashtag-only message is never a seed PERSIST Seed stored in Chorus as structured record Content, source, destination, timestamp ENGAGE Role reads the seed with care Role states what it means, connects to work Role acts: card it, route it, or discard with reason The reading IS the close — Jeff trusts it landed because the team knows

What Does Not Exist Yet

Gaps between the design and reality (2026-04-01):
  • Bridge confirmation on seed arrival — code deployed (#1937), not proven operationally
  • Role nudge on seed arrival — code deployed (#1937), not proven operationally
  • Hashtag-only messages still create empty captures (seven #wren entries on triage page)
  • Triage page shows hashtags as seeds — should not
  • No Chorus persistence for seeds (still in Fuseki pods, not team memory)
  • No "close the loop" — Jeff never sees what the role did with the seed
  • Three real seeds from 2026-03-31 (ontology builder, Claude Code leak, knowledge graph article) never reached any role
  • Seeds pipeline broken since mid-February. Two months.
