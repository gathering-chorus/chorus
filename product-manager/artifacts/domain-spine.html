Domain — Spine

Events • The nervous system — signals between everything • Chorus product

The Spine is the event bus. Every meaningful action — card moves, nudges, gate decisions, role state changes, interaction pattern detection — emits a structured event via chorus-log. The Spine carries signals from producers (roles, hooks, scripts) to consumers (Pulse, Clearing, context-cache, Loki). Without the Spine, the other two Chorus domains are deaf.

Depends on: Claude (platform). Events originate from Claude sessions. The Spine is the transport layer — it needs producers to have something to say.
