Domain — Photos

Harvesting • Collections • Life value stream

200K+ photos spanning Jeff's entire life. Three eras of capture, two primary sources (Apple Photos, Google Takeout), face clustering, and a canonical graph that reconciles across sources. The largest collection domain by volume after music.

Three Eras

Pre-2006 — No digital

Scanned physical photos. Picasa provenance: camera → Windows PC → Picasa → Google Photos. Only surviving copies for this era.

2006-2007 — Camera era

Digital camera before iPhone. Photos live in Google Takeout exports.

Dec 2007-present — iPhone era

Apple Photos is authoritative source. iPhone since December 2007. Apple Photos SQLite is the source of truth for this era.
