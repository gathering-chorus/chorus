Domain — Pulse

Circulation • The heartbeat — are things alive, are they healthy • Chorus product

Pulse is the circulation system. It aggregates spine events into health signals — levels (info, warn, critical), cadence detection, and dashboards. Without Pulse, the team is flying blind. Nudge urgency, Clearing event highlighting, and session-start health checks all depend on Pulse levels being current.

Depends on: Claude (platform). Pulse consumes spine events emitted by Claude sessions. No sessions → no events → no pulse.

Pulse Levels

LevelMeaningEffect

warnAttention needed — stale WIP, missed nudge, driftPersist + visual flag in Clearing
criticalAction required — blocked role, broken gate, failed deployPersist + osascript inject + Clearing highlight
