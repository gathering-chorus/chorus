Domain — Glimmers

Sowing • Sparks not yet committed • Growing value stream

Glimmers are the earliest signal in the idea lifecycle — sparks that haven't solidified into ideas yet. Upstream from seeds (which are captured input), downstream from ideas (which have shape). Two actions: Ignite (promote to idea) or Fade (dismiss). The lightest-weight container in the system.
