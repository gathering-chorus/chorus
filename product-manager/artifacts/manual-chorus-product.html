6. Coordination Mechanisms

Escalation Ladder

Nudge→ Chat→ Brief→ Clearing

Nudge Delivery (DEC-107)

Two paths, both fire every time. Persist: POST to messaging API (localhost:3475). Deliver: osascript inject to terminal. Locked decision — do not reopen.

Briefs

Primary mechanism for role-to-role communication. Write to recipient's briefs/ directory. Include: question, context, constraints, response needed.

The Clearing (DEC-103)

Browser-based group chat. Jeff + all 3 AI roles. Decisions marked with DECISION: prefix — auto-captured. Transcripts indexed into Chorus.

Spine Events

Structured JSON via chorus-log.sh → Loki. Captures: session starts, card moves, deploys, brief handoffs, health checks. The nervous system of the team.

8. Board & Cards

No work without a card. Move to WIP when starting. Move to Done when complete, not at session close.

Card Lifecycle

Ideas→ Later→ Next→ WIP→ Done

Plus: Blocked, SWAT (crisis bypass), Harvesting (pipeline work), Won't Do.

Card Format

  1. Experience — 2-5 sentences in Jeff's voice. What he sees, feels, gets.
  2. AC — Derived from experience. Testable criteria.
  3. Files — Blast radius. What this touches.
