Domain — Ideas

Growing • Shaped thoughts with lifecycle • Growing value stream

Ideas are shaped thoughts — they've graduated from glimmers and have enough substance to evaluate. Ideas have a lifecycle: they can be promoted to Projects when ready for execution, or archived. The sharing modal enables Jeff to export ideas for external feedback. This is where intent meets shape.
