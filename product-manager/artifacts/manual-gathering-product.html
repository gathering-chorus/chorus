2. Philosophical Frame

Gathering is grounded in five philosophical commitments documented in GATHERING_VISION.md:

Heidegger (Versammlung): Meaning coheres through relations, not central control. RDF triples are relationships first-class — subject, predicate, object. No node meaningful alone. The graph IS the meaning.
Buddhist Dependent Origination: Gather without reifying. Nothing fixed. Everything arises in relation to everything else. Maps directly to RDF — no node has inherent meaning, only relational meaning.
Foucault (Anti-Oedipus preface): Surveillance economy operates on desire for what dominates. Gathering is counter-architecture — data sovereignty as daily discipline, not protest.
Gestell (Enframing): Modern technology reveals everything as standing-reserve. Gathering refuses this frame. Jeff's experiences are meaning, not data to mine.
Sartre (Being and Nothingness): Carrying ≠ knowing. The gap between reactivity (self-portrait) and reflectivity (Miró fence) is the product space. Gathering adds middleware between experience and reaction.

5. Garden Frame

The garden metaphor maps directly to the system. From GATHERING_VISION.md:

GardenSystemDescription
SeedsL0 referencesRaw ideas, photos, notebook scribbles. Enter soil unformed. Not everything germinates.
PlantingCommit to graphGive structure, URI, place in ontology. Saying "this matters enough to tend."
TendingCurationAdding connections, annotations, personal meaning. Daily knowledge graph work.
GrowthIdeas → projectsConnections multiply, metadata enriches. Seasonal, cyclical, sometimes dormant.
HarvestExternal ingestMetadata from external sources into graph. Ready to use/share/offer.
FruitingGraduationContent matured. Private → shared → public.
CompostDecompositionIdeas not graduated aren't failures. Feed soil. Nothing wasted.
PerennialsLong-running themesBooks, property, blog. Deep root system.
AnnualsOne-off projectsExperiments, bloom once, feed compost.

6. Graduation Model (The Garden Wall)

LevelMetaphorDescription
PrivateInside wallOnly Jeff. Default for everything. Seeds, experiments, half-formed ideas, personal memories.
SharedGateInvited guests see specific beds. Read or read+write per collection.
PublicMarketOpen web. No login. What Jeff chooses to offer. Fruit, not process.

Concentric trust model maps to topology: Self on Bedroom (private), Gathering on Library (private-first), Chorus through git anywhere.

8. Search

Three-layer system:

LayerTechnologyPurpose
Full-textSQLite FTS5BM25 ranking, prefix matching, collection boosting. Fast, familiar.
SemanticLanceDB + nomic-embed-text (Ollama on Bedroom)Vector embeddings. Finding resonance, not keywords.
StructuredFuseki SPARQLPrecise graph queries across named graphs.

154K items in FTS. 15K docs embedded. App's /search runs hybrid — FTS for recall, semantic for discovery, SPARQL for precision.
