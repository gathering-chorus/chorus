Phase Vocabulary — RESOLVED — Jeff confirmed 2026-04-01

Jeff's resolution: The two phase vocabularies are not in conflict — they are two different value streams. The OWL already has 3 ValueStream instances. Each domain can belong to one or both Gathering streams.
