Domain — Interactions

Communication • The shapes of how roles talk to each other and to Jeff • Chorus product

Interactions is the domain that owns every multi-role communication protocol. Nudges, demos, pairing, chat, pipelines, gemba, briefs — each is a distinct interaction shape with its own rules, participants, and artifacts. The Clearing is the venue where some of these happen live; this domain defines the shapes themselves.

Depends on: Spine (events) for signaling. Clearing (coordination) as a venue for live interactions. Pulse (circulation) for urgency routing on nudges.
