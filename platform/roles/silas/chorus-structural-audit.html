HIGH (14) — Real gaps requiring work

NEW: Gathering App is a God Service (17 domains)
Single Express app serves stories, music, photos, people, property, garden, and 11 more domains. No blast radius isolation. Any route/handler change risks all domains. Surfaced during today's ontology session — Jeff confirmed this is a structural problem, not just scale.
NEW: Messaging Tier is a Single Point of Failure
Nudge delivery, Clearing/Bridge, Chorus API, and all role coordination depend on one SQLite-backed service. No redundancy, no failover. Surfaced during today's design session.
NEW: Tool ≠ Service ≠ Deployment (grain error)
Jeff's correction: the ontology conflates tools, services, and deployments. Fuseki is a tool AND a service AND a deployment. These are three distinct concepts that need separate modeling. Impacts card routing and service design accuracy.
NEW: Domain → Phase affinity, not Domain → Stream
Current ontology maps domains to value streams. Jeff's correction: domains have phase affinity (e.g., seeds lives in Capturing, not "the Chorus stream"). Impacts workflow automation and card routing.
NEW: 79% practices ungated
Only 6 of 34 practices have automated enforcement. The other 28 run on discipline only. Today's design session quantified this — it's worse than the original 7 high-fanout count suggested.
Blog domain: Active with testCount=0
Blog is Active but has zero tests. The wordpress-harvester.service.ts exists but is untested. Needs a card.
Clearing / Bridge: High blast radius rewrite
disposition=rewrite with 2 dependsOn (Messaging Tier, Chorus API). WF-160 complete (shims thinner) but still needs full rewrite. Session Tailer and Daily Review depend on it downstream.
7 ungated high-fanout practices
Convergence (3 domains), /demo, /acp, /clearing, /nudge, /chorus, Infra Alert (2 domains each) — all touch multiple domains with no automated gate. Some may already have partial hook coverage that needs wiring via hasGate.
