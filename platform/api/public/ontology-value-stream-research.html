Part 2: Public Ontology Landscape survey

Layer 1 — Well-Served (Reuse These)

MFO — Mental Functioning reuse
OWL · OBO Foundry · BFO-aligned
Covers attention, cognition, consciousness, perception, memory, reasoning. Realist ontological stance — things that exist, not just labels.
github.com/jannahastings/mental-functioning-ontology
👉 Foundation for modeling attention (Spark) and cognitive processes (Triage, Transform).
MFOEM — Emotion Ontology reuse
OWL · Extends MFO · OBO Foundry gold standard
Full emotion/mood/affect spectrum with formal definitions. Emotions, moods, appraisals, subjective feelings.
obofoundry.org/ontology/mfoem
👉 Could feed Mood Ring (#278). Models the affective dimension of Reflecting.
PROV-O — Provenance reuse
OWL2 · W3C Recommendation
Entity-Activity-Agent model. Tracks lineage: how was this produced? What activity generated it? Who was involved?
w3.org/TR/prov-o
👉 Essential for insight provenance — "how did this understanding arise?" Tracks the lineage of self-knowledge.
BDRC/BDO — Buddhist Digital reuse
OWL/RDF Turtle · Very active · 28M+ pages
Person-Work-Place-Event for Buddhist textual heritage. Excellent Linked Open Data design patterns. Tibetan, Pali, Sanskrit, Chinese.
github.com/buda-base/owl-schema
👉 Design patterns for Buddhist concept vocabulary. Models texts about concepts, not the concepts themselves.

Layer 2 — Partially Served (Adapt These)

HASIO — Affective States + Influences adapt
OWL · University of Ottawa
Models influences ON emotional states: personality, needs, subjective well-being. Modular design. Includes recognition methods.
github.com — HASIO
👉 Closest to Self domain territory. Models conditionality — what influences what. Maps to dependent origination thinking.
BDI — Belief-Desire-Intention adapt
OWL · 2025 paper · LLM integration
Models agency and intentionality. Bridge between "I notice this" (reflection) and "I intend to act on this" (understanding). Logic Augmented Generation for LLMs.
arxiv.org/abs/2511.17162
👉 Bridge between Reflecting and Spark (the cycle). LLM integration directly relevant to Chorus.
EFO — Emotion Frame Ontology adapt
OWL · DOLCE-aligned · 2024
Emotions as semantic frames: who feels what, why, toward what. Not just taxonomy — models the structure of emotional experience.
arxiv.org — EFO
👉 Perfect for story-driven self-understanding. A story isn't an emotion label — it's the whole frame of experiencing.
OntoLife — Biographical Events adapt
OWL-Full · 2009 · Extends FOAF
Person-Period-Event model. A life is a series of events situated in periods — exactly how jb:Story works. CV-focused, no interior life.
Springer — OntoLife
👉 Good skeleton for Self domain. Extend with affective, reflective, and contemplative dimensions.

Layer 3 — Not Served (Gathering Builds These) novel

Buddhist Philosophical Concepts build
No public RDF/OWL ontology exists.
Dependent origination (pratityasamutpada), Four Noble Truths, Eightfold Path, five skandhas, twelve nidanas, jhana states. BDRC models texts about these — nobody has formalized the concepts themselves.
👉 Genuine contribution to the field. Academic meditation taxonomies (Three Tier, Jhana Classification, Phenomenological) provide structure ready for OWL formalization.
Contemplative Experience States build
No formal ontology for lived practice.
Meditation, somatic awareness, presence, attention quality. MFO covers cognition but not contemplative states. The Three Tier taxonomy (concentration → observation → inquiry) maps to the value stream.
👉 Maps to Jeff's Life's Practice: Flexibility/Equanimity → Mindfulness/Presence → Reflection/Agency/Possibility → Learning.
The Meaning Bridge build
Observation → Reflection → Understanding.
The right side of the value stream. QS ontologies model measurement. Emotion ontologies model feelings. Nobody models the process of turning observation into self-knowledge.
👉 Gathering's original contribution. This is what makes it a self-understanding tool, not a capture tool.
The gap: Public ontologies cover what you feel (MFOEM), what you did (OntoLife), what you tracked (QS), and what texts say about Buddhism (BDRC). Nobody covers how noticing becomes understanding. That's the meaning bridge — and it's what the right side of Gathering's value stream does.

Part 3: Academic Meditation Taxonomies ready for formalization

These papers provide rigorous structure but haven't been expressed in RDF/OWL. They're the raw material for a SKOS concept scheme.

Taxonomy Source Structure Gathering Mapping
Three Tier Classification PMC 2023 Concentration/focused attention → Passive observation → Visualization → Contemplation → Introspection → Inquiry Concentration → Observation → Inquiry mirrors Capture → Revisit → Reflect
Jhana Classification Springer 2024 Unified account of absorption states. Common features across meditation manuals. Systematic definition of jhana 1–8. Deep states of concentrated awareness. The "Transform" stage in contemplative practice.
Phenomenological Classification PMC 2022 Activity-based: what the practitioner does, not what tradition they follow. Activity-based framing matches Gathering's verb-first value stream (noticing, preserving, sorting...)

Part 4: Recommended Architecture for #74

4
The Meaning Bridge build — novel
Gathering's original contribution. Classes connecting observation → reflection → understanding. The process of turning noticing into self-knowledge. Integrates Life's Practice ontology.
3
Buddhist Concept Scheme build — SKOS
Formal vocabulary for dependent origination, skandhas, jhana, Eightfold Path. Draw on BDRC design patterns + academic meditation taxonomies. Genuine public contribution.
2
PROV-O — Insight Provenance import
W3C standard. Track how understandings arise: what activity, what entity, what agent. Every reflection has lineage.
1

OBO Foundry standard. Attention, cognition, consciousness, emotion, mood. The substrate of inner experience.
Key insight: Layers 1–2 are imports — stable public standards. Layer 3 is a genuine contribution (Buddhist concepts in OWL don't exist). Layer 4 is what makes Gathering Gathering — the meaning-making cycle formalized.
