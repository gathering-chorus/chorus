Bridwell Consulting

Three streams. One practice.

LLC — Roslindale, Jamaica Plain, Brookline

One LLC, three revenue streams, complementary rhythms. Chorus Playbook delivers fractional CTO diagnostics for growing tech companies. Chorus Consulting builds technology for local businesses year-round. Light Life Urban Gardens designs gardens with people in spring and summer. All three feed the same practice — listening, assessing, building things that last. Powered by the same AI team and knowledge graph underneath.

Chorus Playbook

Year-round — 1–2 engagements per quarter

Fractional CTO diagnostic for fast-growing small and medium companies. An 8-section framework — business model, org design, cost inventory, tech stack, value delivery — delivered through structured interviews, task-based discovery, and findings synthesis (Debt, Performance, Opportunity, Risk).

Powered by Chorus: the AI team runs the diagnostic, captures findings in the knowledge graph, delivers a professional DPOR report. First engagement: run the diagnostic on our own system — borging the playbook through Chorus to prove it works.

Chorus Consulting

Year-round — limited availability in summer

I help small local businesses navigate technology transitions — new stores, new systems, new ways of reaching customers. Your data, your hardware, no cloud lock-in. I work with an AI team to move fast and keep costs low.

Discovery session → technology roadmap → system setup → monthly support. Whether it's POS and inventory for a farm opening a new store, or a yoga studio wanting to own their booking data. Retainer clients get year-round support.

Light Life Urban Gardens

Spring & Summer

I show up, walk through your garden, mostly listen. I draw and take notes. You get a hand-drawn map of your space with ideas and next steps.

Consultation → seasonal design package → collaborative build days. Not a landscaper — a garden partner.

Gathering + Chorus

Year-round investment

The platform underneath. A personal knowledge graph and AI team coordination system. Every consulting engagement makes it better. The consulting IS the product work.

Go to Market

This is a neighborhood business. Your market is people who know someone who knows you.

  1. Word of mouth from pilots Akasha, Allandale, Parkway tell their people
  2. Vision pages as your website gathering-chorus.html, chorus-consulting.html, lightlife.html — already built
  3. Local presence Roslindale Farmers Market, Allandale bulletin board, JP community boards, Brookline Booksmith
  4. Hand-drawn garden maps as marketing Post a few with permission. People see the map and say "I want that for my yard."
  5. Nextdoor — Roslindale / JP / Brookline One post about garden consultations will get responses

You don't need a marketing budget. You need 5 good conversations with people in the neighborhood.
