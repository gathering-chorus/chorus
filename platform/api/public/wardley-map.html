What the Map Tells Us

Jeff's Time Is the Scarcest Component

It sits at Genesis, powering both streams. The seasonal calendar is how you manage this constraint — garden presence in summer, consulting bandwidth in winter. You can't scale Jeff. You scale the platform underneath him.

The Consulting Differentiator Is Custom, Not Commodity

"I help you own your software" is not "I set up WordPress." The knowledge graph, AI team roles, and SOLID pods are what make Chorus Consulting different. Moving those toward Product (repeatable, forkable) is how consulting scales without everything being custom.

Garden Consultation Stays Genesis on Purpose

Hand-drawn maps, personal presence, listening first. That IS the value. Don't try to productize it — people are paying for Jeff's eye and Jeff's attention. The constraint is hours in the day, not process efficiency.

The Chorus Protocol Is the Strategic Bet

It's Custom-Built today, moving toward Product. When it's forkable and documented enough that a client can run it (DEC-057: product maturity threshold), consulting scales beyond Jeff's personal bandwidth. Every client engagement makes the protocol better. The consulting IS the R&D.

Where Things Are Moving

Chorus Protocol → Custom to Product. Each consulting engagement tests and hardens the protocol. The goal: a client can fork it and run their own version with minimal Jeff involvement.

Knowledge Graph → Custom to Product. The ontology is stabilizing (v1.1.0, 12 domains). Harvest pipelines becoming repeatable. A new client could get a working knowledge graph in days, not weeks.

Garden consultation → Stays Genesis. This is intentional. The hand-drawn map is the artifact, Jeff's presence is the product. Scale through reputation and referrals, not automation.

AI Team Roles → Custom to Product. Wren, Silas, Kade are proven patterns. A client could get their own role configuration from the same CLAUDE.md fragment system. 59 decisions worth of encoded judgment.

What to Build Next

The map says: invest in the Custom-to-Product movement. Every hour spent making the Chorus Protocol more forkable, the knowledge graph more repeatable, and the AI roles more configurable directly increases consulting leverage.

Don't invest in making commodity components better — Docker, Prometheus, and Grafana are good enough. Don't invest in productizing the garden consultation — that's Genesis by design.

The $60K target is achievable when the Custom-Built layer moves far enough right that a consulting engagement is 20% custom and 80% configured. That's the Y2–Y3 inflection point.
