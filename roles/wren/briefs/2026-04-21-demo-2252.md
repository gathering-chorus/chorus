**Builder:** Kade

## Why this matters
Before this, agents asking current-state questions had to hit 7 different paths under different envelope shapes (/api/chorus/freshness raw JSON, /api/chorus/quality/summary bare object, board data not queryable at all, coverage not surfaced anywhere). Now every current-state question has a stable `/api/chorus/context/*` path with one envelope shape — step+product+domain+subdomain+timestamp+source+data. Agents can cite responses unambiguously; consumers don't guess at shape per endpoint.
