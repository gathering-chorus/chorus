## Why It Matters

Before this card, a coverage drop from a test deletion, fixture change, or config edit was invisible until someone noticed manually. Now at 02:30 every night the system measures every TS project and Rust crate against Jeff-authored floors and posts a Bridge summary. Regression is the headline; numbers are the evidence.

## What Jeff Sees

**On regression:**
```
NIGHTLY COVERAGE REGRESSION — 2026-04-19
  FAIL directing/products/cards: 75.0% (floor: 79.0%, delta: -4.0%)
  OK: directing/clearing: 87.0%, platform/chorus-hooks: 50.0%
```

**On green:**
```
NIGHTLY COVERAGE PASS — 2026-04-19
  directing/clearing: 87.0%, directing/products/cards: 80.0%, ...
```
