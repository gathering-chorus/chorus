## Why this matters

Closes the third-order gap Silas flagged in #2210 review. A `.test.ts` file whose only tests were parameterized (`test.each` / `it.each` — common for handler input-table tests and edge-case matrices) silently passed the #2196/#2210/#2215 gate because the parser didn't recognize the curried-call form.
