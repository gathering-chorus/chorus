# Demo Brief — #2277 Remove app-level activate from chorus-inject

**Builder:** Silas  
**Card:** [swat] Remove application-level activate from chorus-inject script  
**Status:** Done  

## Why this matters

Every tool call from any role fired `activate` on the Terminal application, yanking it to front. Three roles active = Terminal thrashed repeatedly during away windows — sessions unresponsive on return.

## What shipped

Removed `activate` from `build_inject_script` in `chorus-inject/src/lib.rs`. Now uses `set frontmost of w to true` (window-scoped only). Binary rebuilt and signed.

## Proof

Three-role round-trip nudge test: wren and kade both confirmed delivery clean with no focus steal. Kade verified: System Events `keystroke` targets process by name, not OS frontmost — set-frontmost-only is correct.

## Kade feedback

Two low-risk gaps noted: (1) activate assertion is fragile to comments/varnames; (2) no integration test for inject-while-backgrounded. Approach sound.
