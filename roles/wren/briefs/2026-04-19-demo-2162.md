## What was broken

`/borg/replay/` rendered a blank session list for 43 days. `/api/chorus/sessions` returned `{"sessions":[]}` despite 109 session files existing on disk. The #2124 deep-health probe surfaced this on 2026-04-17.

## Root cause

`chorus-api/src/session-replay.ts` had `MAX_SESSION_AGE_MS = 7 days`. The newest session file is from March 7 (43 days old). All 109 files were being filtered out as too old.

## Fix

Changed `MAX_SESSION_AGE_MS` from 7 to 90 days in `platform/api/src/session-replay.ts`. Rebuilt and restarted chorus-api.
