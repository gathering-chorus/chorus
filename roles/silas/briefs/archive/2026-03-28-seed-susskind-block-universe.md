## Key points from Susskind

1. **There is no universal "now."** Simultaneity is relative. Different observers at different velocities have different definitions of what's happening "right now."

2. **The block universe:** Past, present, and future all exist equally in the four-dimensional structure of spacetime. Your entire world line — birth to death — is a static structure. Every version of you exists at its coordinates.

3. **"Now" is neurological, not physical.** Your brain constructs the sensation of the present moment. Reality doesn't have one. Every cross-section of your world line experiences itself as "now."

4. **Death is a boundary, not annihilation.** Your world line ends, but the coordinates that already exist stay. You exist eternally at those spacetime coordinates.

5. **You can affect what future coordinates look like, but you can't change finished coordinates.** Causality flows one direction, but the structure is already there.
