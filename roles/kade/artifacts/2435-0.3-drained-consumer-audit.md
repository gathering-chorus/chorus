## Collision risk (direct ┬¦1 concern)

**┬¦1 AC proposes**: receiver emits `nudge.acknowledged` when it surfaces a nudge in the envelope.
**Current code**: `role_state.rs` drain emits `nudge.acknowledged` with `count={N}` payload.

During the NUDGE_PATH_V2 parallel-run phase, **both paths emit `nudge.acknowledged`** with different payload shapes. Anything that starts consuming the new event (pulse telemetry, Clearing UI, future consumers) sees mixed-semantic events. Options:
- (a) Namespace new event differently (`nudge.v2.acknowledged` or `nudge.surfaced`).
- (b) Retire role_state drain *before* new path lands (breaks stdout-delivery on idle transition; may need replacement).
- (c) Accept collision during parallel phase; require payload differentiation to filter.

My read: (a) is cleanest ŌĆö rename new event to `nudge.surfaced` (what it means semantically anyway) and free `nudge.acknowledged` for the drain, or rename the drain event since that's the one with fewer consumers (0 production).
