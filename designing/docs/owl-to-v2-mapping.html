OWL (athena + borg) → Athena v2 baseline

how the legacy OWL classes (chorus: structural + borg:) land on v2 (the 33 domains + the 4 axes). v2 is the target; this is what migrates into it.

Two layers. The OWL has spine classes (which become v2's structure) and entity/facet classes (which become typed content inside a v2 domain). A third bucket maps to nothing clean — that's the drift to resolve.
