OWL Vocabulary

OWL vocabulary: A Product (Gathering, Chorus) is owned by Jeff. A Sub-product (Cards, Clearing, Hooks) is owned by a role. A Domain is a bounded context with data, handlers, and routes. A Service is running code that operates in a domain. Roles own sub-products, domains, and services. The ontology governs — SPARQL queries validate completeness.

Quality Gap

27 of 40 domains have zero tests. 7 are TESTED (search, cards, nudge, gates, BDD-suites, photos, demo). 6 have SOME. The quality contract can't enforce what isn't measured. Next steps: register 15 unregistered domains in the API, set a quality floor per domain tier (REAL domains must have tests).
