Layer 1 governs Layer 2. The conversation pattern determines which card gates activate. A Demo pattern triggers the proving gate. A Direction pattern triggers pull and build. A Story pattern triggers no gates — just capture.

Layer 2 feeds Layer 3. Every gate emits spine events. Every mutation through board-ts produces structured events that flow into the Chorus index.

Layer 3 feeds back to Layer 1. Borg reads the event stream and surfaces what the data says: pattern distribution, duration trends, transition health, gaps. The team learns how it operates by observing its own signal.

Concurrent Patterns

Jeff operates in multiple patterns simultaneously across roles. This is the goal, not an edge case.

Three roles, three patterns, one person. Jeff directs from conversation, not from coordinating sequential tasks. The roles hold their own state; Jeff moves between them fluidly.

Concurrent patterns are healthy. They mean the system is absorbing coordination. The anti-pattern is Jeff stuck in one pattern because the other roles can't proceed without him.

Desk freedom test: Can Jeff step away from the computer and the roles keep working? Concurrent patterns are evidence that the answer is approaching yes.

The andon should measure what kind of attention Jeff is spending, not just how much.

What Borg Will Surface

With enough instrumented data, the three-layer architecture reveals how the team actually operates.

Distribution

What percentage of interactions are Direction vs. Ideation vs. Reflection? A team that's 90% Direction and 0% Reflection is executing without learning.

Duration Trends

Demos getting longer? Something's wrong upstream (AC quality, smoke check skipped). Stories getting shorter? Jeff might be editing himself.

Transitions

Ideation → Direction is healthy (ideas graduating). Ideation → Ideation → Ideation with no Direction means nothing's landing. SWAT → SWAT → SWAT means systemic quality problems.

Gaps

No Story pattern in two weeks. No Reflection in a month. The absence of a pattern is data too.

Correlation

Do sessions that start with Triage produce more completed cards? Does Gemba reduce SWAT frequency? Does Ideation volume correlate with shipped features two weeks later?
