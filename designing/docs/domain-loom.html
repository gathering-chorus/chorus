Domain — Loom

Chorus • How the team operates • Toyota loom, jidoka, lean (DEC-041)

Loom is the organizational backbone — the structures that make the team legible to itself. Named after Toyota's automatic loom that stops when a thread breaks (jidoka). Eight sub-domains covering how the team decides, tracks, practices, depends, governs, staffs, skills, and serves.
