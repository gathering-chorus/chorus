Athena

Athena is the structural and query layer of Chorus \u2014 the canonical model of what exists in the team's product world (products, domains, services, value-streams) and the predicates that connect them.

ownerrole-wren
statusbuilding
audienceGates, SessionStart envelope injection, blast-radius, role-nudge routing, demo feedback, the tagging layer, and Jeff and the team via the rendered tree page.

Borg

Borg is the self-judgment product of Chorus, spanning Proving and Reflecting \u2014 the system seeing itself so no human has to be the eyes.

ownerrole-silas
statusbuilding
audienceThe roles (to self-coordinate and catch a dark peer, flapping service, or drift before Jeff does) and Jeff (outcomes only \u2014 a calm board, or the single thing that needs him).
value propBorg's product isn't dashboards \u2014 it's Jeff's attention back: it surfaces the one thing that needs a person, calmly, to the owner, so no human is ever the first to notice a stall.
visionA model-driven reflect cycle where monitor and alert are ontology domains whose contracts generate, the page renders a verdict not a readout, and the capability travels generatively to run with chorus off.
gapsProbes were bespoke and lied, there is no liveness or crash-loop signal, alerts are hand-routed, there is no model-driven contract, and there is no Borg page \u2014 the observation surfaces are scattered.
