Observability — Service Design

2026-04-06 — Silas (#2273). Source: shared-observability repo + chorus alerting scripts + deep-health inventory.

Silas can diagnose any infrastructure failure without asking Jeff. A role can determine "is the system healthy?" from any session. Alerts route to roles, never to Jeff. When observability is down, the team is blind.
