Wren + Jeff · 2026-05-14 · in progress · intent-set mapping (not graph-derived)

Structure: Chorus product → value-stream step → subproduct → domains. The five value-stream steps (Shaping, Designing, Directing, Building, Proving) hold subproducts; each subproduct holds the domains that carry its content. ? marks an unresolved placement.

analytics? and metrics? under Borg are unresolved. The customer-happiness conversation raised whether measurement of customer outcome belongs under Borg (PROVING — empirical measurement) or under Loom (SHAPING — operating-model memory, "is the product working for the customer"). The tree captured the question; it has not been settled.
