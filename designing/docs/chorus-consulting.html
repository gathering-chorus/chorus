I bring 25 years of enterprise architecture experience to small business problems. I've led engineering organizations of 100+ people at companies like Staples and CVS Health. Now I apply that same rigor — systems thinking, quality gates, structured decision-making — at a scale where it can make a real difference for one business at a time.
