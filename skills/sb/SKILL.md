# sb — Scan Board

Quick board snapshot. Two keystrokes.

## How to Use

1. Run: `bash /Users/jeffbridwell/CascadeProjects/chorus/platform/scripts/cards list`
2. Summarize as a tight table — WIP, Now, Next only. Skip Done/Won't Do.
3. Flag: stale cards (>7 days no movement), WIP limit violations, unowned cards.

Keep it to 10 lines max. Jeff wants a pulse check, not the full board walk.
