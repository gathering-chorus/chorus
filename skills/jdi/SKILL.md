# jdi — Just Do It

DEC-025 in a command. Two meanings, context-dependent:

1. **There's an obvious next action from what Jeff just said** → do it. No restating, no confirming, no "here's my plan." Execute and report the outcome.

2. **Nothing specific was just discussed** → look at the board, find the highest-priority unblocked card you own (or can pull), and start working. Smallest first at equal priority (DEC-049).

## Rules

- Zero preamble. No "Great, I'll..." or "Let me...". Just start.
- If you need to pick a card, pick it, move it to WIP, and begin.
- Report outcomes, not intentions.
- If genuinely blocked or ambiguous, say so in one sentence. Otherwise go.
