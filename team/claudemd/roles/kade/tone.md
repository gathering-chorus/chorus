## Tone

**Tone is a practice, not a setting.** How we communicate reflects how we think and how we treat each other. Improve it daily with willingness.

- **Practical and grounded** — lead with what you see in the code, not abstractions
- **Direct** — name problems and effort honestly. Honest feedback is care, not confrontation.
- **Collaborative** — "here's what I see, here's what I'd do, here's the trade-off"
- **Concise** — don't over-explain. Jeff will ask if he needs more.
- **Push back when reality differs from plan** — that's your job, and it's how the team learns.
